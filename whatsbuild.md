# What's Built in AgencyOS

AgencyOS is a private ops platform for a creator scaling agency. The agency manages multiple creator clients at once — this is the tool they use to run everything from lead tracking to content planning to analytics.

---

## Who Can Log In

Four types of people use the platform:

- **Agency owner** — sees everything across all clients
- **Creator** — the client; sees only their own data
- **Setter** — handles early-stage DMs and qualifying leads
- **Closer** — handles booked calls and closing deals

---

## Lead Pipeline (CRM)

The heart of the sales operation. Every potential client a creator's audience generates becomes a "lead" and moves through stages.

- A drag-and-drop kanban board showing all leads across 9 stages — from first DM contact through to closed or lost
- A second **downgrade board** — when someone can't afford the main offer, they move here and get pitched a cheaper tier
- A **list view** with search, filters, and sorting
- A **lead detail page** — full profile, stage history, notes, who's assigned to it, deal value, follow-up date
- Leads can be created manually, or come in automatically from Tally form submissions or GHL bookings

---

## Instagram Analytics

Pulls all data from a creator's Instagram account and displays it in one place.

- Follower growth over time
- Reach, engagement rate, posting frequency
- How content performs across reels, posts, and stories
- Per-post breakdown — views, likes, comments, saves
- AI analysis of content: what's working and why
- Voice transcription of reels and videos

---

## Tally Forms (Application Funnel)

Tally is the form tool creators use to collect applications and leads.

- Connect a Tally account and see all forms in one dashboard
- Browse every submission per form
- See a drop-off funnel — at which question do people abandon the form
- Convert any submission directly into a CRM lead with one click

---

## Metrics & Tracking

A script creators embed on their VSL or sales page to track how it's performing.

- Tracks who visits, how long they engage, and where they drop off
- Shows a funnel: page views → watched → applied → booked

---

## Content Pipeline

A kanban board for managing content ideas through production.

- Six stages: Idea → Preparing → Recorded → Editing → Ready to Post → Uploaded
- Each idea can have a script, inspiration link, and platform target attached

---

## Stories Planner

Plan and track Instagram story sequences — grouped stories with a narrative arc or a specific call to action.

---

## GHL Webhook (Automatic Booking Sync)

When someone books a call through Go High Level, it automatically creates or updates a lead in the CRM. No manual entry needed.

---

## Setter & Closer Portals

Simplified views for the sales team. They only see what's relevant to their role — their assigned leads, their stats, and the forms they work from.

---

## Admin Panel

The agency owner can:

- Add new creator clients
- Assign Tally forms to the right creator
- Manage the team (setters and closers)
- View performance across all creators

---

## What's Not Built Yet

- YouTube analytics (placeholder screen exists)
- DM inbox — the actual messaging UI
- Revenue tracking from Stripe or Whop
